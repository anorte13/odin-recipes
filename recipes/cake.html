Cake

Delicous cake that can be yours if you're willing to put the work in to it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 stick of butter

Steps:

  1. Add dry ingredients to a large bowl and mix until evenely mixed.
  2. Put mixed ingredients into a pan and bake at 450 degrees.
  3. Let cool down for 15 minutes
