Lasagna

Description:

The perfect lasagna recipe made with parmesan ricotta cheese filling, melted mozzarella cheese, lasagna noodles, and a robust tomato meat sauce. It is the best lasagna recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 small Onion
  • 1 lb. Ground Beef
  • 3/4 teaspoon of Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 can of Marinra Sauce

    • Parmesan Ricotta Cheese Filling:

    • 2 cups of Whole Milk
    • 1 cup of greated cheese
    • 1 large egg
    • 1 teaspoon of Dried Basil

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and cook for about 3-4 minutes. Add ground beef and increase heat to medium-high. Cook until the meat begins to brown, about 5 minutes. Spainkle with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Lower heat to low and add marinara sauce. If you want the lasagna to be extra saucy, I suggest adding more sauce. Let the sauce continue to simmer.

    3. Parmesan Ricotta Cheese Filling:

    1. In a small bowl, stir together ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese, egg, basil, salt, and pepper.
    2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
